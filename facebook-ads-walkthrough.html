2

Five campaigns, each with a job

The account is organized around real homeowner situations, not one generic "we do roofs" campaign. Each campaign carries its own message and its own intended action.

Local Authority

Owner-led education, local project evidence and the company mission.
→ Watch, remember, engage

Storm Response

Useful guidance after hail, wind or visible damage, timed around Central Texas storms.
→ Call or request an inspection

Aging Roof

Repair versus replacement, warning signs and financing questions.
→ Request the guide or an assessment

Roof & Solar

Detach and reset, warranty protection and coordinated project planning.
→ Request a roof + solar consultation

Follow-Up

FAQ, project proof, the e-book and customer evidence for people who engaged.
→ Return and schedule when ready

The Content Engine

The owner records practical videos on a phone with a simple script. A few batched sessions become weeks of content. Clear captions, one straightforward call to action.
→ Local and natural, never corporate

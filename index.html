Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

Does answering the questions commit me to anything?+
No. This helps us understand your situation so the first conversation is actually useful. There is no obligation, no listing of your information for sale, and no pressure to move forward.
Are you an insurance company or a public adjuster?+
No. Texas Roof Guardians is a roofing contractor. We document your roof's condition thoroughly and support you through the claim process, but decisions about your claim rest with you and your insurance carrier. We don't promise claim approval, and nothing here is insurance or legal advice.
What does a documented inspection include?+
A complete look at the roof system, photos of what we find, and a written condition record you keep. If a claim is involved, that documentation is prepared so it can support the adjuster process.
What if I have solar panels?+
That's one of our specialties. Roof work on a home with solar has to be coordinated: detach, roof replacement, reset, and verification that the system is producing again. We manage that as one project with one accountable company instead of leaving you to referee between a roofer and a solar installer.
What areas do you serve?+
We're headquartered in San Antonio and serve Austin and all of Central Texas.
How fast will someone contact me?+
You'll get an immediate confirmation when your request comes in, and a fast follow-up to get your inspection or review scheduled. If you have an active leak or storm emergency, call 830-228-6123 directly. Storm response runs 24/7.
